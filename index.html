What Synapse Does

needs to operate.

Not another note-taking app. A complete knowledge pipeline from source to memory.

AI Generation from Any Source

Paste text, drop a PDF, link a YouTube video, or pull from your library. Synapse synthesises knowledge into structured study material using Gemini or your own API key.

BYOK or Hosted
🔄

Bidirectional Sync

Write in Joplin, Obsidian, or Notion — Synapse keeps your flashcard decks in sync automatically. Import your existing Anki library, study in Synapse, export back as .apkg. Your notes and your memory, connected.

Sync Pro+
⚡

FSRS-Powered Scheduling

The Free Spaced Repetition Scheduler — state-of-the-art algorithm that schedules reviews based on your actual retention, not fixed intervals. Remembers what you forget.

Chat with Your Library

Ask questions across your entire indexed knowledge base. RAG-powered chat pulls the most relevant context from your PDFs, docs, and notes to give grounded answers.

BYOK or Hosted AI

20+ Themes & Card Families

Cosmic, Catppuccin, Ayu, Solarized, and more. Card theme families from light to premium. Even a custom hex colour theme so Synapse looks exactly how your brain wants it.

Image Occlusion

Annotate anatomy diagrams, circuit schematics, and any visual — hide and reveal regions as study prompts. Create natively in Synapse, import from Anki, export back as .apkg, and repair migrated cards with the mask editor.
